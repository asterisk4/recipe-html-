Chocolate Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together.
  3. Mix in the sugar and eggs.
  4. Fold in the flour.
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking tin and bake for 25 minutes.
